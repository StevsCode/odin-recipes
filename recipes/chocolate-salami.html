Chocolate Salami

Description

With no necessity of baking, chocolate salami is a delicious homemade cooking made with chocolate and dry biscuits.

It is easy to make and ready to be enjoyed by yourself or with your family!

Ingredients

  • 260g dry biscuits
  • 220g melted chocolate
  • 160g butter
  • 80g powdered sugar
  • 30ml milk
  • 15g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Work the softened butter in a bowl until it becomes creamy, then add the powdered sugar and mix carefully.
  2. Add the melted chocolate and stir with a spatula.
  3. Incorporate the eggs and milk.
  4. Sift the unsweetened cocoa powder and mix again.
  5. Add the broken dry biscuits and mix with a spatula.
  6. Shape the mixture into a cylinder using parchment paper.
  7. Wrap it tightly in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
  8. Once chilled, remove the chocolate salami from the wrap, dust with powdered sugar, slice and serve!
