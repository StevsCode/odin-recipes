Microwave Mug Cake

Description

Ready in just two minutes: easy, tasty and light, this microwave mug cake is as delicious as the original! (Recipe for one)

Ingredients

    2 eggs 2 tablespoons brown sugar 3 tablespoons flaxseed oil 200g flour 220ml skimmed milk Baking powder

Steps

    Beat the eggs with the sugar and oil Gradually add the flour and milk, and finally the baking powder Mix until the batter is smooth and fluffy Preheat a microwave-safe mug for one minute Pour the batter onto the mug and cook for two/two and a half minutes Garnish with chocolate, whipped cream and fruit or just maple syrup!
