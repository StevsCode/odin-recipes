Baked Pasta

Description

Easy, quick and convenient to prepare even in advance!

It is full of flavor and will make you and all your guests happy :))

Ingredients

  • 400g mezzi rigatoni
  • 250g sausage
  • 650ml tomato puree
  • 200ml bechamel
  • 300g mozzarella cubes
  • 50g grated cheese
  • 1/2 onion
  • Extra virgin olive oil (as needed)
  • Salt (as needed)

Steps

  1. Pour a generous amount of oil into a large saucepan and pan-fry the chopped onion. Once it's golden and slightly softened, add the crumbled sausage and cook it until browned.
  2. After a few minutes, add the tomato puree, stir and season with salt.
  3. Bring salted water to a boil in a pot, then cook the rigatoni for a few minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta, still al dente, and add it directly to the saucepan.
  5. Add half of the grated cheese and bechamel and mix everything well.
  6. Layer the pasta in a baking dish, adding cubes of mozzarella.
  7. Add more bechamel and grated cheese, then layer the pasta again.
  8. Continue layering with mozzarella, bechamel, and cheese until all ingredients are used. Bake in a preheated static oven at 200 Celsius degrees for 20 minutes. Then, set the grill function for 5 more minutes for a crispy crust.
  9. The baked pasta is ready, gooey and delicious.
  10. Serve the pasta on individual plates, top with more grated Parmesan and enjoy!
